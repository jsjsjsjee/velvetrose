About Us

A Luxuries Hotel
with Nature

At The Velvet Rose, we believe that luxury and nature go hand in hand. Nestled amidst breathtaking landscapes, our hotel offers an unparalleled experience where elegance meets the tranquility of the great outdoors. Each of our rooms is designed to provide a serene escape, featuring stunning views of lush gardens and pristine natural surroundings.

We Serve Fresh and
Delicious Food

Indulge in a culinary experience like no other at The Velvet Rose. Our menu features a delightful array of dishes, crafted with the finest ingredients to tantalize your taste buds. From exquisite appetizers to decadent desserts, each meal is a celebration of flavor and creativity. Enjoy a dining experience that perfectly complements the luxurious ambiance of our resort.

Our Mouth-Watering
Dishes

Galbi-Short ribs, often marinated and grilled, considered a special occasion dish.
Bulgogi-Thinly sliced marinated beef, usually grilled or pan-fried, often wrapped in lettuce with gochujang.
Samgyetang-A whole dish stuffed with ginseng, garlic, and other ingredients, traditionally eaten in summer.
Kimchi Jjigae-A hearty stew made with kimchi, tofu, pork, and a spicy broth.
Japchae-Stir-fried glass noodles with vegetables, considered a classic Korean dish.
